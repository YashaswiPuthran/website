I am SO excited to share with you that the My Little Food Critic App is now available WORLDWIDE on the Apple App Store & Google Play Store, it is packed full of brand new recipes, and will be updated monthly with NEW content

I have been working on (relentlessly!) in this app for the past year! It's taken over a year to develop and I am so so proud of the fantastic functionality it offers, the design and of course all of the exciting new recipes!

As well as recipes, there is a weekly meal planner, you can automatically create a shopping list from the recipes and add your own essentials, you can save your favourites and switch between metric and imperial units. I’m so excited for you to discover the flavoursome, nutritious and delicious recipes in this app.

I know so many parents struggle with finding recipes to suit dietary and allergy requirements, so I’ve worked really hard to offer alternatives for the majority of the recipes and cater for different dietary needs. There is an advanced search function so you can filter recipes, for example, gluten free, egg free, dairy free etc.

There are also articles on helpful topics as well as play and learn activities 🙌🏼

I wanted to create this app to make feeding your family easy, accessible and enjoyable whilst packing in as many nutritious ingredients as possible!

You can see a little video about the app below, highlighting all of the key features.

KEY APP FEATURES

  • Brand new exclusive recipes
  • New content added monthly
  • Advanced ‘easy search’ functionality - search for recipes with ease using ingredient keywords or by recipe title and/or filter your recipes based on dietary/allergy info (nut free, dairy free, egg free, gluten free, vegan and vegetarian)
  • Shopping list - automatically add recipe ingredients to create a shopping list. You can also add all your family essentials to your list.
  • Meal planning feature – add recipes to create a weekly meal plan
  • Instructional recipe videos for selected dishes
  • Ability to change between metric & imperial units
  • Customize your own favourites list with your most-loved recipes
  • Recipes divided into: Breakfast, Main Meals, 10 Minute Meals, Sides & Snacks, Sweet Delights, No Bake Snacks, Snacks Under 5 Minutes, Lunchboxes & Days Out
  • Lifestyle section with articles from industry experts covering a diverse range of topics.
  • Play & Learn section with activities to support learning through play

The app is £1.49/$1.49 use (or currency equivalent) a month, and every month the app will be updated with band new recipes and additional content!
